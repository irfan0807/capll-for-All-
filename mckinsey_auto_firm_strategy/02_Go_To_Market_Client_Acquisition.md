# 02. Go-To-Market & Client Acquisition

**Confidential Document**  
**Prepared by:** Strategic Consulting Group  
**Subject:** B2B Sales Playbook for Automotive Engineering Services

## 1. Identifying the Ideal Customer Profile (ICP)
* **Tier-1 Suppliers:** Bosch, Continental, Magna, Visteon, Aptiv, Denso.
* **Tier-2 Tech Providers:** Companies supplying specific software stacks (e.g., AUTOSAR stacks like Elektrobit or Vector).
* **EV Startups & OEMs:** Rivian, Lucid, BYD, Nio, and traditional OEMs transitioning to EVs.

**Target Personas:** Director of Engineering, V&V (Verification & Validation) Manager, System Test Lead. *(Do not target HR or Procurement initially).*

## 2. The Inbound "Authority" Engine
Clients in automotive engineering buy trust and technical competence. 
* **The GitHub Moat:** Publish open-source, sanitized tools. Example: A Python script that auto-generates structural CAPL test cases from an Excel/DBC file. 
* **Technical Whitepapers:** Publish highly specific guides on LinkedIn. Example: *"Handling SecurityAccess (0x27) in UDS using Python and CANoe."*
* **Webinars:** Co-host webinars demonstrating modern HIL CI/CD pipelines.

## 3. Targeted Outbound Strategy (The Trojan Horse)
Automotive sales cycles take 6–18 months. Accelerate this by offering a low-risk, high-reward entry point.

### The "Test Automation Audit" Pitch
**Message Template (LinkedIn/Email to a V&V Manager):**
> "Hi [Name],
> I noticed [Company] is scaling its ADAS validation team and aggressively hiring CANoe/CAPL engineers. Often, teams face bottlenecks where manual testing slows down the nightly release cycle.
> My firm specializes exclusively in migrating automotive Tier-1s to 100% automated vTestStudio/CAPL pipelines. We’re offering a complimentary 2-week 'Test Automation Audit'—we will take one of your manual test specs and build an automated PoC framework to show you the exact ROI in man-hours saved. 
> Open to a 15-minute technical intro call next Thursday?"

## 4. Strategic Partnerships
* **Tool Vendors:** Become an official integration partner with Vector Informatik, dSPACE, or Intrepid. When they sell a $100k hardware rig to a client who doesn't have the engineers to use it, the vendor will recommend your firm.
* **Network Leverage:** The founders must initially leverage their own previous industry contacts to secure the first 1-2 anchor clients.
